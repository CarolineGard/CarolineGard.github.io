Explore Motion

My parts in the project “Explore Motion”, lead by Samuel Fast and Emil Rydberg. The brief was to make an installation for Hyper Island´s annual tech exhibition at Nobelberget in Stockholm. My group created an Arabic theme installation, where the user meet a genie after rubbing a physical magic lamp, and is thereafter presented the story of the genie’s journey. The installation was projected on a thin fabric to create a holographic and mysterious feeling.
